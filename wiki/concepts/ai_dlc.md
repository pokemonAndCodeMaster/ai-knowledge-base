# AI-Driven Development Life Cycle (AI-DLC)

## Definition
AI-DLC is a software development methodology designed to optimize the synergy between human intelligence and AI agents. It shifts the primary engine of development from "Human implementation" to "Human orchestration of AI implementation."

## Core Principles
1. **AI-First Generation**: AI agents are the primary authors of code and documentation.
2. **Pace Optimization**: Restructuring team habits to match the near-instantaneous output of AI (solving the "Pace Disparity").
3. **Intent-Driven Engineering**: Developers express high-level intent; agents handle the mechanical details.

## Evolution from Agile
| Feature | Traditional Agile | AI-DLC |
|---------|-------------------|--------|
| Primary Actor | Human Developer | AI Agent |
| Human Role | Implementer | Editor/Orchestrator |
| Sprint Cycle | 1-2 Weeks | 1-2 Days (Bolts) |
| Work Item | Epic/Story | Unit of Work |
